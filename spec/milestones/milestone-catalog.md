# Milestone catalog — startups under ~2 years

This catalog is part of **Startup Expectations Specifications**. Common goals early teams work toward. Each item has **one primary bucket** (`funding`, `building`, `customers`, `trust`, `operations`, or `team`) in this repo; real life often touches two—pick the main question you are trying to answer.

**Maturity-first path:** if you only care about **pre-seed → seed → Series A**, use the ordered lists in **`../maturity/`** (`pre-seed.md`, `seed.md`, `series-a.md`) instead of this category-first catalog.

---

## Fundraising & runway (`funding`)

- Raise pre-seed, seed, or extension; define amount and use-of-funds story  
- Hit milestones meant to unlock the **next** round (traction, product, team)  
- Model runway: burn, hiring plan, buffer  
- Investor pipeline: intros, diligence readiness, light data room  
- Grants, accelerators, strategic investment (if relevant)  
- Non-equity options: venture debt, revenue-based financing, cloud credits  
- Unit economics experiments: gross margin, payback—not only top-line growth  
- Board or investor reporting rhythm once investors are in the cap table  
- Exceptional but real: wind-down or acqui-hire optionality (runway and legal reality)

---

## Operations & back office (`operations`)

- Cap table hygiene: early splits, option pool, SAFEs vs priced round  
- Company financial backbone: entity, bank, payroll, basic accounting rhythm  
- Runway dashboard and burn reviews  
- Customer and vendor contracts: templates that match your stage  
- Insurance when it matters (e.g. D&O, EPLI)  

---

## Build & ship (`building`)

- MVP scope and “done enough to learn”  
- MVP / private beta / public launch  
- Technical foundation: repo, CI, environments, staging, baseline security  
- Reliability and support at the right level—not over-built  
- Product surface area: mobile, API, integrations if central to the offer  
- Pivot or scope cut: new ICP, kill a line of work  
- Hardware / physical goods: supply chain, certifications, returns  
- Deep tech / R&D milestones: lab, trials, regulatory path  

---

## Customers & revenue (`customers`)

- Name ICP (ideal customer) and wedge  
- Problem–solution validation: interviews, pilots, LOIs  
- First paying users or design partners  
- Feedback loop: support, usage, NPS, churn signals  
- Positioning: site, one-liner, category story  
- Early GTM: first repeatable acquisition motion (content, outbound, PLG, partnerships)  
- Pricing and packaging experiments  
- First case studies or reference customers  
- Community, ecosystem, or open-source motion (if deliberate)  
- Partnerships and channels: platforms, resellers, API ecosystems  
- Strategic design partners and co-development  
- International or multi-region only when relevant  

---

## Trust, compliance & risk (`trust`)

- IP basics: trademarks, filings when core to the business  
- Compliance **when required** for your market (e.g. HIPAA path, SOC 2 roadmap for enterprise)  
- Security and privacy posture for what you collect  
- Incident readiness: who responds, how you communicate with users  
- Regulatory and industry rules beyond generic “compliance”  
- Ethics and misuse if your product can be abused  
- Dual mission or structure (nonprofit arm, B Corp, climate) if core  

---

## Hiring & team (`team`)

- Founding team roles, equity, and decision rights  
- First hires (often engineering, product, or GTM generalist)—beyond “first developer” as needed  
- Lightweight interview process and bar  
- Onboarding and how you communicate async  
- Advisors vs operators: clear expectations  
- Co-founder alignment: roles, conflict process, departure scenarios  
- Immigration and work authorization  
- Remote and multi-country hiring: payroll, contracts, entities when hiring abroad  
- Operating rhythm: goals, weekly priorities, retros  
- Values and “how we work” at a useful minimum  
- Founder sustainability: cadence and boundaries so the company outlasts burnout
